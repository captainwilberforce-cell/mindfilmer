About

Captain Wilberforce is a Leeds-based band led by singer-songwriter Simon Bristoll. Since their debut Mindfilming (2004), the band has received national airplay on BBC Radio 2, BBC 6 Music, and more, with songs featured on HBO, ITV, and The Fashion Channel.

Current lineup (2025): Simon Bristoll (vocals/guitar), Dave Naylor (bass), Carl Banks (drums), Rob Simpson (guitar), with Geoff Doig recently joining on bass.
