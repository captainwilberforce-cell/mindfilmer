About

Captain Wilberforce is a Leeds-based alt-pop / powerpop band. Since their debut Mindfilming (2004), they have received national airplay on BBC Radio 2, BBC 6 Music, and more, with songs featured on HBO, ITV, and The Fashion Channel.

Current lineup (2025): Simon Bristoll (vocals/guitar), Geoff Doig (bass), Carl Banks (drums), Rob Simpson (guitar).
